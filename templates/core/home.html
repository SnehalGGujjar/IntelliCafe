QR Table Ordering

Customers scan a QR code at their table, browse the menu, and place orders without needing any app or account.

Weather-Smart Menus

The menu automatically highlights items suited to the current weather — hot soups on cold days, cold brews on hot ones.

Instant QR Payments

UPI-ready QR codes displayed at checkout. Staff confirms payment, and the bill is emailed automatically.

Inventory Tracking

Stock levels auto-deduct with every confirmed order. Get alerts before items run out.

Analytics Dashboard

Revenue, top-selling items, order trends — all in one admin view updated in real time.

Staff OTP Login

Employees authenticate with a time-limited OTP sent to email — no weak password risks.
